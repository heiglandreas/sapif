Show content before a post, if the posts position in "The Loop" mathces a given equation

## Description ##

Define content (for instance adverts) that shall be shown between posts
depending on the position in "The Loop".

You want to show something before the second post on your blog? Set the equation
to 'x' and the results to '2' and define your content. That's it!
